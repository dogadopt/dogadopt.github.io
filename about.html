About

Dog Adopt UK

At Dog Adopt UK, we are dedicated to promoting dog rescue over buying dogs. We believe that every dog deserves a second chance at a happy life, and by adopting, you are giving them that opportunity. Dog adopt UK is a non-profit, purely committed to ensuring that the number of dogs in rescue is promoted over the purchasing of dogs. We only add rescues listed on the Association of Dogs and Cats Homes ([ADCH](https://adch.org.uk/)) list, ensuring that all our dogs come from reputable and caring environments. By supporting these trusted rescues, you are helping to uphold the highest standards of animal care and welfare.
